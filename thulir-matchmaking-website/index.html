SECTION 02 — ABOUT THULIR

We believe beginning again is not a compromise — it is an act of courage.

A place where your story is heard before your profile is ever introduced.

Thulir · துளிர்A tender new shoot — the first sign of growth after stillness.

Thulir — meaning a tender new shoot, the first sign of growth after stillness — was built on a simple belief: that every person deserves a second chance at love, and that finding it should feel dignified, safe, and deeply personal.

Thulir is built especially for those who have loved and lost — divorced or widowed adults ready to begin again. Singles who have not yet found the right person are welcome here too.

There is no rush, no judgment, and no algorithm deciding your future.

At Thulir, a real person — your dedicated matchmaker — listens to your story, understands what matters to you, and works with quiet intention to find a connection that feels natural, genuine, and lasting.

This is not matchmaking as a transaction. This is matchmaking as care.

About Thulir

Thulir is a boutique matchmaking service open to all singles aged 25 and above. We welcome never-married individuals, but our heart lies in serving divorced and widowed adults — people who have lived, loved, and are now ready to find meaningful companionship again. We are not a dating app. We are a human-led, concierge matchmaking service where a real person works personally on your behalf.

Matrimonial websites and dating apps put the burden entirely on you — to browse, filter, message, and hope. Thulir is different in three fundamental ways. First, a dedicated matchmaker does all the work for you — no swiping, no endless scrolling. Second, every introduction is handpicked with care and made only after both parties give consent. Third, we stay with you throughout — refining every match based on your feedback, supporting you at every step. This is not a platform. This is a relationship.

Every introduction is made only after both parties give explicit consent. No personal details are shared without your knowledge.
