FOUNDER'S STORY

Why I built Thulir.

A personal belief that relationships can begin with less interference, more self-knowledge, and real human care.

Founder, Thulir
Healing taught me to listen before I act.
“
Finding a partner — the first time, or after loss — can be dignified, intentional, and free of the noise.
— Founder, Thulir
The story, in three moments.
01 · The training02 · The observation03 · The belief
01
The training

I am a practicing pediatric surgeon by training — but somewhere along the way, I became just as invested in the science of relationships as I am in the science of healing.

02
The observation

Over years of clinical practice, I watched something else unfold quietly around me: marriages ending, families fracturing, and often, well-meaning relatives and parents shaping decisions that were never truly theirs to make.

03
The belief

In India, love rarely happens in isolation — families, society, and circumstance all have a hand in it.

04
The reason

I started Thulir because I believe it doesn't have to be this way. That finding a partner — the first time, or after loss — can be dignified, intentional, and free of the noise.

05
The intention

Thulir is my attempt to change how relationships begin in India: with less interference, more self-knowledge, and real human care guiding the way.

— Founder, Thulir

A quieter, more thoughtful way to begin.
